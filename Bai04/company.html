technology and cost management solutions for employers & others

ezURs.com making decisions EZ®

We provide technology and cost management solutions for employers, insurance companies, third party administrators, and government agencies. Understanding the complex challenges of managing claims costs without disrupting the quality of care is important us. Through this we are able to apply the most efficient solutions for your cost management needs. We have adopted specific business disciplines using technology, intelligence rules to streamline the decisions for managing claims. With a healthy technology platform we have the ability to offer you a one of a kind experience that is sure to make your claims management EZ.

Our services combined with technology solutions make the intricacies in healthcare management for workers’ compensation, health, disability and auto liability EZ.

Dr John Yatsu (MD, MBA, FACS, FAADEP)

Dr Yatsu presently serves as Medical Director for ezURs.com in Texas. John has years of experience working as trauma surgeon and served as medical director for multiple managed care companies. John has instrumental in developing quality programs with focus on utilization review.

Dr. Jeff Harris (M.D., M.P.H., MBA)

Dr. Harris serves as Medical Director for ezURs.com in California. He has worked as a Primary Treating Physician, corporate medical director, Senior Vice President of a workers’ compensation insurer, and as a utilization reviewer. He wrote and edited the first edition of the ACOEM Occupational Medicine Practice Guidelines, and served as associate editor of the second edition and methodologist for the third edition. He was principle investigator for the Robert Wood Johnson Foundation Workers Compensation Health Initiative projects on guideline dissemination and URAC Performance Measure Development and Testing. Dr. Harris has worked closely with state governments and private industry to use evidence-based best practices to improve management and outcomes of injured workers.
